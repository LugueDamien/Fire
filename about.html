History

Understanding the history of fire prevention is critical to appreciate how far we have come in terms of fire safety and prevention. Fire prevention efforts date back centuries, with various societies developing their approaches to combating fires.

In ancient Rome, for example, fire brigades were established to respond to fire emergencies. In the 17th century, the Great Fire of London prompted the development of fire insurance and the installation of fire hydrants. In the 19th century, the industrial revolution led to a significant increase in fires, resulting in the formation of the first professional firefighting organizations.


Fire Prevention Month

Fire Prevention Month is an event that takes place every March in the Philippines to raise awareness about fire safety and prevention. It is a time for individuals, communities, and organizations to come together to educate people on how to prevent fires and minimize their impact.

During Fire Prevention Month, various fire departments, schools, and community organizations host events and activities to promote fire safety education. These may include fire drills, safety demonstrations, and workshops on fire prevention techniques and equipment.
